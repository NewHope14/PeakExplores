About Brahmatal Trek

The Brahmatal Trek is a snow lover’s paradise located in Uttarakhand, offering a surreal winter experience with sweeping views of Mt. Trishul and Nanda Ghunti. This moderately easy trek leads to the enchanting frozen Brahmatal Lake surrounded by snow-covered trees and meadows.

The trail winds through ancient oak and rhododendron forests and opens to vast alpine clearings covered in pristine snow. Perfect for beginners and nature lovers, the Brahmatal trek showcases Himalayan beauty at its purest.

Starting from the village of Lohajung, the journey also unveils hidden viewpoints like Jhandi Top and Bekaltal. The experience is both peaceful and photogenic — a winter postcard brought to life.

Itinerary

Reach Lohajung and prepare for the trek. Overnight stay in guesthouse.

Trek through oak forests to reach the beautiful Bekaltal campsite.

Snow-laden trails lead you to Brahmatal Lake nestled in the Himalayas.

Ascend to Jhandi Top for panoramic views, then return to Brahmatal.

Retrace your route through forests to reach Lohajung.

Say goodbye to the snowy haven and begin your journey back home.
