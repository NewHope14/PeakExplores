About Kanakata Pass Trek

The Kanakata Pass Trek is a captivating 12-day Himalayan expedition that begins from Delhi and takes you deep into the lesser-explored trails of Uttarakhand. Nestled in the Bageshwar region, this trek is perfect for those who crave solitude, untouched nature, and authentic mountain experiences far away from crowded tourist circuits.

As you move through the trail, you’ll witness an ever-changing landscape—from the oak and rhododendron forests around Karkiya to the rustic charm of Jatoli village, where time seems to slow down. Gradual ascents lead to alpine meadows bursting with wildflowers, glacial streams, and serene campsites tucked under star-lit skies.

The highlight of the journey is reaching the Kanakata Pass, located at an elevation of 14,921 ft, offering panoramic views of snow-capped peaks and surrounding valleys. The climb to the pass is both challenging and deeply rewarding, making it an ideal choice for trekkers with moderate experience who want to push their limits and connect with nature in its rawest form.

This trek is not just about reaching a summit—it’s a cultural and environmental immersion. Expect to interact with local shepherds, explore traditional Himalayan life, and travel through forests that echo with the call of Himalayan birds and wildlife.

Best seasons for this adventure are June, September, and October, when the weather is most stable and the views are crystal clear. With transport from Delhi by Tempo Traveler and customizable dates, this trek fits well into a flexible schedule for individuals, groups, or customized expeditions.

Whether you’re looking to escape the routine, challenge yourself, or simply bask in the grandeur of remote mountain terrain, the Kanakata Pass Trek promises an unforgettable experience off the beaten path.

Itinerary

Travel to Haldwani from Delhi. Dinner & Overnight stay at Haldwani.

Travel to Bageshwar from Haldwani. Dinner and Overnight stay at Bageshwar.

Trek 4 km from Bageshwar to Karkiya. Dinner and overnight stay.

Trek from Karkiya to Jatoli. Overnight stay & dinner at Jatoli.

Trek from Jatoli to Khatliya. Dinner & overnight stay.

Trek from Khatliya to Baloni top. Steep climb. Overnight stay.

Trek from Baloni top to Devikund after breakfast.

Trek from Devikund to Bhanar. Rest and overnight stay.

Trek across Kanakata Pass and descend to Patharkani.

Trek from Patharkani to Jatoli. Long descent day.

Trek to Dhakuri. Dinner & Overnight stay.

Return to Bageshwar. Overnight stay and dinner.

Travel back to Delhi. End of trek.

⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTE

  • This is a challenging high-altitude trek that demands good physical fitness and prior multi-day trekking experience. Prepare with regular cardio and strength training at least 4–6 weeks before the trek.
  • The route includes steep ascents (like the 70° climb to Baloni Top) and remote campsites — mental preparedness is key.
  • Weather can be unpredictable, especially at higher camps. Trek itinerary may shift due to safety concerns, snow, or rainfall.
  • Mobile network is unreliable beyond Bageshwar. Inform family in advance and set expectations for communication blackout during the trek.
  • Only limited medical facilities are available en route. Carry your own basic first aid and prescribed medicines.
  • Always carry proper trekking gear including layered clothing, waterproof shoes, rain protection, woolens, and a headlamp.
  • Maintain eco-friendly practices: do not litter, avoid plastic, and help protect local flora and fauna.
  • Respect local traditions, customs, and follow your trek leader's instructions strictly.
