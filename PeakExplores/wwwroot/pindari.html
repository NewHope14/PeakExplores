About Pindari Glacier Trek

The Pindari Glacier Trek is a rewarding Himalayan journey ideal for beginners and families. This trek winds through the Kumaon Himalayas, offering lush forests, village hospitality, and the stunning view of the Pindari Glacier snout.

The route runs along the Pindar River with scenic halts at Dhakuri and Khati villages, leading to Zero Point where the glacier view is most majestic. Trekkers can spot peaks like Nanda Devi, Maiktoli, and Panwali Dwar.

This trek is perfect for those seeking adventure with comfort, cultural interaction, and manageable altitudes. A soulful introduction to Himalayan trekking!

Itinerary

Arrive at Loharkhet via Almora, briefing and rest.

Moderate climb through forest trails, stay at Dhakuri.

Descend to Khati village, enjoy warm hospitality.

Trek through meadows, riverside walks, night at Phurkia camp.

Witness the glacier at Zero Point, then return to Khati.

Return journey via shared transport. Trip ends.
